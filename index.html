Support Privacy Policy

About the App

One of Us is a playful finger picker for group decisions. Everyone places a finger on the screen, the countdown begins, and one player is randomly chosen.

The app is designed for fast, local, in-person use on iPhone and does not require an account to play.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026

One of Us - Finger Picker does not collect, store, track, or share personal data.
